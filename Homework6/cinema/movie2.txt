
- "CODA" is a 2021 American drama film written and directed by Sian Heder.
- The title stands for "Child of Deaf Adults."
- The film follows Ruby, a CODA, as she navigates her life as the only hearing person in her Deaf family.
- "CODA" premiered at the 2021 Sundance Film Festival, where it won the Grand Jury Prize and Audience Award.
- The film received widespread critical acclaim for its performances, writing, and portrayal of Deaf culture.
- "CODA" was acquired by Apple for distribution and became the first film at Sundance to be sold for over $25 million.
