"Sweet Nothing in My Ear"

- "Sweet Nothing in My Ear" is a 2008 television drama film directed by Joseph Sargent.
- The film stars Jeff Daniels and Marlee Matlin as parents struggling with the decision to give their Deaf son a cochlear implant.
- It explores the Deaf community's divide over the use of cochlear implants and the impact on family dynamics.
- Marlee Matlin, who is Deaf herself, brings authenticity to her role and sheds light on the complexities of Deaf culture.
- The film received mixed reviews, with praise for its performances but criticism for its handling of the cochlear implant debate.
