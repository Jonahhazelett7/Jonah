
- "Sound of Metal" is a 2019 American drama film directed and co-written by Darius Marder.
- The film stars Riz Ahmed as a drummer who begins to lose his hearing.
- Ahmed's portrayal earned him widespread acclaim and nominations for the Academy Award for Best Actor.
- The film explores themes of acceptance, adaptation, and the Deaf community's culture.
- It received critical acclaim for its authentic representation of Deaf culture and its innovative sound design.
- "Sound of Metal" won two Academy Awards for Best Sound and Best Film Editing.
